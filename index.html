MY DASHBOARDS

Power-Bi Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive visualization of key business metrics across multiple dimensions. It highlights top-level KPIs, including a total profit of $1.5M, total sales of $14M, and an average discount of 0.05, suggesting that discounts are minimally used. Technology leads in both sales (40.12%) and profit ($886.3K), although its profits have been declining over time, raising concerns about cost management. The West and Ontario regions are top sales performers, while regions like Nunavut lag behind. Corporate customers generate the most sales at $5.5M, with opportunities to expand into the small business and consumer segments. Additionally, furniture has strong sales but low profit margins, indicating a need for cost or pricing adjustments. Overall, the dashboard offers actionable insights for optimizing sales strategies and improving profitability across various categories and regions.

Excel Dashboard

The dashboard provides a clear overview of customer spending and sales performance for a business. On the left, a bar chart lists the 10 least loyal customers, showing how little they’ve spent. This helps the business identify opportunities to engage these customers and figure out why they aren’t spending more. On the right, a pie chart breaks down sales channels, highlighting that 30% of sales come from kiosks and 39% from convenience stores. This information helps the business see where most sales are coming from and decide where to focus their efforts. Overall, the dashboard is a useful tool for making decisions that improve customer loyalty and drive sales growth.

Tableau Dashboard

I analyzed key business metrics, including profits, discounts, sales, and shipping costs, using Tableau for visualization. The analysis revealed that Manitoba led in profits with $136K, while Pens & Art Supplies had the highest discounts at $60.53. Technology was the top profit-generating category, contributing 58.24% of total profits, and sales peaked in September and March, with dips in April and July. Additionally, Small Box containers generated the highest sales at $5.09M, and the West region had the highest shipping costs at $24.6K, providing valuable insights to inform business strategies.
